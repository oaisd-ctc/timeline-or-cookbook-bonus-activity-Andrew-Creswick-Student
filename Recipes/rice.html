Rice!

Rice is a very good food that is simple and quick to make.

  1. Rice
  2. Water

Instructions:

  1. Pour Rice and Water into a pan.
  2. Heat it up till its boiling.
  3. Turn heat down to Low.
  4. Let it sit for several minutes.
  5. After water is gone, turn off heat, move pan off heating spot, let it steam for several more minutes.
  6. Enjoy!

Information

Serving Size: Bowl of rice

Cook: 15 mins

Steam time: 5 min
