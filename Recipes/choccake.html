Chocolate Cake!

A chocolate cake is a delightful desert. It is sweet and rich; perfect for a birthday party or celebration.

  1. 1 3/4 cups Flour
  2. 3/4 cups Coca Powder
  3. 1 3/4 cups ggranulated Sugar
  4. 2 teaspoons Baking Soda
  5. 1 teaspoon Salt
  6. 1/2 cup Oil
  7. 2 Eggs
  8. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  9. 1 cup Buttermilk
  10. 1 cup coffee

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix all the ingredients
  3. Divide batter between pans. Bake for 23-26 minutes.
  4. Remove cakes from the oven and cool them.
  5. Make the buttercream
  6. Assemble and frost
  7. Refrigerate uncovered cake for at least 30-60 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!

Information

Serving Size: Serves 12-16

Prep: 30 mins

Cook: 25 mins
